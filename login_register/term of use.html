Selsal Policy

Please read these terms of service carefully before using the website operated by Selsal.


Personal Information:


All personal information you provide us with or that we obtain will be handled by Selsal as responsible for the personal information. The personal information you provide will be used to ensure deliveries to you, the credit assessment, to provide offers and information on our catalog to you. The information you provide is only available to Selsal and will not be shared with other third parties. You have the right to inspect the information held about you. You always have the right to request Selsal to delete or correct the information held about you.


Payment Options:


You can choose from the following payment option:


  • Cash on Delivery:

    • We use Cash on Deliver (COD) payment option for orders placed in Cairo, Giza and Alexandria. You can pay cash while receiving you shipment that is to be delivered through a shipping courier. For more details about shipping see Shippingsection below.


  • Credit Card:

    • All transfers conducted through Selsal are handled and transacted through third party dedicated gateways to guarantee your protection. Card information is not stored and all card information is handled over SSL encryption.


  • Pay in Store:

    • You can pay cash in our store located in 261 L Hadayek Al Ahram. After placing you order and choosing pay in store option, you’ll receive a call from us confirming your order and the pick-up day, so that you can pick up you order from us and pay in the store.



Shipping:


  • -   Shipping within Cairo, Giza and Alexandria is done through a shipping courier. Shipments are delivered within 5-7 days after ordering.

  • -   Shipping to governorates other than Cairo, Giza and Alexandria is provided though Egypt express mail. And payment should be sent in advance through Vodafone cash. For more details you can call us on +201015109677 or email us atinfo@selsal.online . Shipments are delivered within 5-7 days after payment.

  • -   We ship internationally through the Egyptian Express Mail (EGYMAIL). Shipment takes 7-10 days to be delivered; according to the country.

  • -   Shipping costs and payment fees are recognized before confirming the purchase.

Order Confirmation:


An order confirmation will be emailed to you within one business day of placing your order. If you do not receive a confirmation email, please check your spam folder to make sure it hasn’t been filtered.


We also call our customers to confirm the orders within one business day of placing the orders. And for any concerns you may also call us at +201015109677or email us at info@selsal.online to confirm your order.


Changes & Cancellations:


Call or email us right away if you need to make a change to your order. Once an order has been shipped changes cannot be made.


Returns & Exchanges:


You can return any purchased item if it’s not a personalized item (gifts with names or customized writings); excluding the original shipping cost as well as the shipping cost to send the replacement. If you wish to return a personalized item we offer exchange only; excluding the original shipping cost as well as the shipping cost to send the replacement.


All sale and discounted items and Special Orders are final sale. Returns must be sent in new, unused condition with original tags attached and in original packaging within 3 days of receipt of package. Please send us the unused item(s), packaged properly, and sent prepaid. Return is done through the same method of shipping to our address:
